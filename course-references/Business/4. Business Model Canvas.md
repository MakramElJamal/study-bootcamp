Three Questions:

Is it desirable?

Is it feasible?

Is it viable

FEASIBILITY

The ability of the business to deliver on its value proposition through resources, activities, and partners

DESIRABILITY

The attractiveness of the business to customers; it focuses on customer needs and wants

VIABILITY

The financial sustainability of the business, focusing on revenue streams and cost structure
