Whatis PEST Analysis?

PESTSTANDSFOR POLITICAL, ECONOMIC, SOCIAL,AND TECHNOLOGICAL FACTORS. It helps entrepreneurs understand external factors that can affecttheir startup.

PEST analysis is especially useful when evaluating a new market or designing a business model .

Political Factors

-GOVERNMENTPOLICIES AND REGULATIONS

-Political stability and support for entrepreneurship

-Legal barriers orincentives

-Public funding and access to licenses

Economic Factors

-INFLATIONANDINTEREST RATES

-Employment/unemployment levels

-Consumer purchasing power

-Availability of capital and credit

Social Factors

-CULTURALVALUESANDATTITUDES

-Demographics and population trends

-Education and literacy levels

-Lifestyle changes and social trends

Technological Factors

-ACCESS TO INTERNETAND MOBILE

TECH

-Infrastructure and connectivity

-Emerging technologies

-Digitaltools and platforms
